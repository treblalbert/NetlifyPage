About Bald Ball

Bald Ball is a difficult physics-based game where you control a man in a hamster ball climbing the world to reach the moon made of cheese. Will you master the power of the hamster ball?

Physics-Based Gameplay

Master realistic physics as you roll, bounce, and climb through challenging environments.

Unique Premise

Help our bald protagonist reach the moon made of cheese in his trusty hamster ball.

Challenging Levels

Navigate through increasingly difficult obstacles and terrain on your journey to the moon.

Steam Release

Available now on Steam with full controller support and achievements.

About Albert Freeman

I'm a 27 year old game developer from Barcelona. I make Unity games. My first commercial game, Bald Ball, is published on Steam, and I share other experiments on itch.io.

Right now I'm leading development on Mushy Roots, a metroidvania I'm building with a digital artist and a music composer. I handle most of the development while coordinating our small team.

I studied data analytics at Ironhack Bootcamp and built some Python + AI tools to make data analysis easier. That project is paused while I finish the Mushy Roots demo. I'm also learning C++ from scratch using Bjarne Stroustrup's book, which has been the best learning experience so far. C++ is just the start of my path to becoming a software engineer.

In my free time I like making music, painting with oil, bouldering and hiking, though I don't have much time for those things right now.
