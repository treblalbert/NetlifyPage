BENEATH THE GARTEN OF BANBAN

DOCUMENT TYPE: INVESTIGATION SCHEMATICS

Core Premise

You are a freelance investigator tipped off about a hidden access route beneath the facility seen in Garten of Banban. The email includes sewer schematics and a promise of payment for proof. You are entering through something that was never meant to be used again.

Core Pillars:
  • Bodycam immersion (No HUD beyond recording overlay)
  • Light investigative mechanics & Short puzzles
  • Unique Drone Gimmick
  • Limited Battery & Limited Stamina (No Combat)
  • High tension chase sequences & Tragic ending
CURIOSITY ➔ UNEASE ➔ SURVIVAL ➔ DESPERATION ➔ INEVITABILITY

Act 1: The Sewers

LEVEL 1 – THE HIDDEN ROUTE

Setting: City sewer system. Wet concrete, dripping pipes, distant industrial hum.

Gameplay:

  • Tutorial movement and bodycam interface.
  • Automated Text Update: "You’re close" (includes map of the sewers).
  • Drone Introduction: You can switch perspectives between Investigator and Drone.
Puzzle: Navigate the labyrinth until you reach a closed gate. Switch to Drone mode, fly through a small vent, and open the gate from the other side. Once you enter, the gate closes behind you.

Act 2: Abandoned Med Wing

Theme: This is the "Infrastructure." No longer a public kindergarten.

Rooms: Storage, Generator Room, Med Bay, Security Room.

Puzzle: Restore Power

  • Find fuel canisters. While carrying one, you cannot sprint.
  • Something begins roaming while you are encumbered.
  • First Colored Keycard: Use drone to retrieve a missing keycard (iconic to Banban).

Enemy 1: Frowny

Concept: Tall slim white mascot with unsettling blank expression.

Behavior: Roams slowly. Cannot hear; only sees straight forward. If it spots you, it kills you instantly.

Exit: Escape Frowny via an elevator after the locked door.

Act 3: Prototype Classrooms

Theme: Experimental testing zone. Furniture bolted upside down, walls covered in failed mascot sketches. Audio logs whisper of discarded prototypes.

Rooms: Prototype Classroom, Observation Rooms, Mascot Storage Closet, Audio-Visual Testing Chamber.

Vent Labyrinth: You find a map of the vents on a big screen. As the character, you guide the drone through the vents to reactivate electrical parts. This restores power and grants access to the next color key.

Enemy 2: Copycat

Concept: A failed Banban mimic experiment. Aggressive mascot.

Mechanic: Pressure plate puzzles. You must solve puzzles to force Copycat to mimic your movements, ultimately leading him to fall into an abyss.

Exit: Reach the next elevator to go deeper into the facility.

Act 4: Research Core

Theme: The heart of the operation. Computers, server rooms, and reality distortion. Shadow figures flicker in and out of existence.

The Chase:

A wall explodes; you must flee from a giant mascot. You escape through a door that is too small for it to enter, arriving at the Server Room.

Server Puzzle: High ceiling room with screens displaying numbers. Only the Drone can see the color differences on 3 specific numbers. You must input these numbers in order (Lowest to Highest) to reveal the next keycard.

Enemy 3: Delta

Concept: Failed mascot/tech hybrid. Extremely glitchy.

Behavior: Follows you only when you AREN'T looking at him. Navigate the labyrinth/server room by moving physics-based objects to block his gaze.

Exit: Escape via the emergency stairwell and lock it behind you with the keycard.

Act 5: Final Escape

Theme: You were never meant to leave. Small corridors and platforming sections with lethal falls.

  • Broken Stairs: Platforming section leads back toward the surface.
  • The Reverse Garden: You enter the original Garten of Banban building, navigating the first level in reverse.
  • Final Boss: A giant, crazy fusion of Delta and Hybrid begins the final chase.

THE FINAL CHOICE

The game pauses. You must choose:

  1. Carry Evidence: Keep the drone and camera.
  2. Abandon Evidence: Drop everything to run faster.

Canonical Death

Too slow while carrying evidence. A mascot hand grabs you. The camera falls, recording your end.

Non-Canonical End

You escape, but the evidence is destroyed by the Fusion. The truth remains buried.

END SCREEN STAT: "You lasted 01:34:12 in the Garten."
