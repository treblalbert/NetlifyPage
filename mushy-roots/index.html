About Mushy Roots

Mushy Roots is a rogue-like metroidvania developed by Albert Freeman, with the help of artist Sergi Batet and musician Ignacio Ramirez.

Right now it's in an early prototyping stage, trying a bunch of different mechanics and stuff to see what's just fun, as I discover what's the route I want this game to have (besides what's already planned like the lore, areas, rogue-like design, etc.).

The current goal is to have a prototype demo ready for December 2025 or January 2026, to then launch with it a Kickstarter and search for proper funding for this project.

About Albert Freeman

I’m a 27 year old game developer from Barcelona who creates Unity games. My first commercial game, Bald Ball, is available on Steam. I also publish smaller experiments on itch.io and on this personal website.

I studied data analytics at Ironhack Bootcamp and am currently learning C++ from scratch using Bjarne Stroustrup’s book, which has been one of my best learning experiences so far.

In my free time I like making music, painting with oil, bouldering and hiking, though I don't have much time for those things right now.
