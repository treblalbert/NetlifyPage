Hi there! I'm Albert, a 27-year-old from Barcelona. I'm passionate about both nature and technology. While I spend my days coding games and learning data analytics with Python, my true passion lies at the intersection of the natural and tech worlds.

Currently, I'm studying and practicing everything related to data analytics and Python, as I'm aiming to build a career in this field.

At the same time, I'm working on a couple of side projects:

  • A fitness/nutrition AI-based app that I initially developed for personal use. I soon realized it had real potential, so I'm refining the main prototype to share it with others who, like me, believe that taking care of your body and mind is the foundation for everything else.
  • A pair of AI-powered productivity tools for data work, one focused on automating and simplifying data cleaning, and another designed to help generate visualizations and plots faster. Both are still works in progress, but they're aimed at boosting efficiency for data analysts and developers.
  • Game development, where I go from prototyping quirky ideas to more serious concepts. I believe creativity should be fun, and I think I've finally landed on a game prototype I'm excited to develop over the long term. It's still under wraps for now, but I look forward to revealing it in the future.

Outside of coding, you'll often find me playing various instruments, creating oil paintings, or planning my next mountain adventure. I live by the philosophy that "If you do well, good things will happen." It's about showing up, doing the work, and trusting the process.
