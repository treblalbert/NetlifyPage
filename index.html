About Me

I'm a 27-year-old game developer and data analyst from Barcelona. You'll always find me working on a random videogame prototype, tho I'm currently full time working on a post-soviet survival game named 'Lenimki'.

My background in Unity development has given me hands-on experience building complete games, from prototypes to published titles. My first commercial release, Bald Ball, is available on Steam, and I regularly share smaller experiments on itch.io. I'm currently working on new gameplay prototypes and developing personal software tools.

I studied data analytics at Ironhack, where I built Python and AI-based tools to streamline analysis workflows. As part of my final project, I designed and analyzed a dataset generated from gameplay interactions, which helped solidify my interest in combining creativity with analytical thinking. I continue to grow as a data analyst by experimenting with diverse datasets and refining my ability to turn raw information into meaningful insights.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy music production, oil painting, bouldering, and hiking.
