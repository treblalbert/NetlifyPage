About Me

I'm a 27-year-old game developer and data analyst from Barcelona. You'll always find me working on a random videogame prototype, though I'm currently full time working on a post-soviet survival game named Lenimki.

My background in Unity development has given me hands-on experience building complete games, from prototypes to published titles. My first commercial release, Bald Ball, is available on Steam, and I regularly share smaller experiments on itch.io.

I studied data analytics at Ironhack, where I built Python and AI-based tools to streamline analysis workflows. As part of my final project, I designed and analyzed a dataset generated from gameplay interactions, which helped solidify my interest in combining creativity with analytical thinking.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy music production, oil painting, bouldering, and hiking.
