Hey there! I'm Albert, a 27-year-old from Barcelona who believes in the power of real food, movement, and mindful living. While I spend my days coding games in Unity and analyzing data with Python, my true passion lies in the intersection of technology and wellness.

I'm currently pouring my heart into developing a fitness app. First for myself, then for anyone who shares my belief that taking care of your body and mind is the foundation for everything else. I'm fascinated by how proper nutrition, consistent exercise, and time in nature can transform not just our bodies, but our entire approach to life. You know that feeling after a good hike? That clarity? That's what I'm chasing.

Beyond the screen, you'll find me playing folk instruments, creating oil paintings, or planning my next mountain adventure. I believe in the philosophy that "if you do well, good things will happen". It's about showing up, doing the work, and trusting the process.

In my free time, I still tinker with game prototypes and weird coding experiments because, honestly, creating stuff is just fun. Whether it's a fitness algorithm or a silly game mechanic, I love bringing ideas to life.
