About Me

I'm a 27-year-old game developer and data analyst from Barcelona. You'll always find me working on a random videogame prototype, though I'm currently full time working on an unannounced FPS game.

My background in Unity development has given me hands-on experience building complete games, from prototypes to published titles. My first commercial release, Bald Ball, is available on Steam, and I regularly share smaller experiments on itch.io.

I studied Data Analytics at Ironhack, where I specialized in building Python and AI-based desktop apps to automatize the process of generating visuals for data analytics. As part of my final project, I created a survivor-likeA survivor-like game is a fast-paced action genre where the player's primary goal is to stay alive as long as possible against overwhelming, ever-growing waves of enemies. Combat is usually automatic or semi-automatic, shifting the player's focus away from precise aiming and toward movement, positioning, and build choices.

These games are defined by a tight feedback loop: you defeat enemies → gain experience or resources → choose upgrades → become stronger → face even denser chaos. game, then I made everyone at school play it. From every playthrough I automatically extracted data to Supabase, which I then used to keep improving this small game. The experiment was successful, and in the end I mixed all my skills from game development to data analytics and the automatization of that whole process.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy music production, oil painting, bouldering, and hiking.
