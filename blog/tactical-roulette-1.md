# Tactical Roulette

Good evening, everyone!

I have some good news and some bad news.

First, the bad news: *Sewer Horror* has been cancelled, at least for now. I struggled to bring it to life in the way I envisioned, and ultimately, a new and far more exciting project came along.

For quite a while, I've been making small, silly mini-games for my friends and me to enjoy on Friday nights while we hang out and have a few drinks. They're usually quick little projects, made just for laughs, that I show off once and then we move on. You can even find some of them on my itch.io page.

The most recent of these Friday-night experiments was *Tactical Roulette*. My original idea was simple: create a Russian Roulette-style game where players could use their webcams to put their faces into the game, making it even more hilarious. The "loser," of course, would have to take a drink.

What started as a quick joke project quickly grew into something much bigger. Since the core concept came together so fast, I decided to expand it by adding cards to make the gameplay more strategic and interesting. I ended up creating around ten cards for that first version.

Then something unexpected happened.

My friends and I kept playing. And playing. And playing. By the end of the night, our eyes were red and itchy from exhaustion. That had never happened before. Usually, these little games entertain us for maybe 30 minutes before we move on to something else, whether that's playing on the Switch or just settling into the kind of wonderfully chaotic conversations that only happen late at night. But this time, we played for over three hours.

That was the moment I knew: this had to be my next Steam game.

So here I am, one month later. The complete Party Mode, including online multiplayer, is finished and fully playable. I've released it as a free demo on Steam, featuring 50 cards, 3 in-game events, and plenty more surprises that I'd rather not spoil. And yes, that's just the demo.

You can check it out here:

[**Tactical Roulette Demo on Steam**](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4605870/Tactical_Roulette_Demo/)

Over the next two months, I'll be developing the full game. The biggest addition will be a roguelite mode, where you'll compete in increasingly dangerous and illegal underground tournaments of Tactical Roulette. Survive, and you'll earn money to buy new card packs, expand your deck, and climb the ranks of this shadowy gambling circuit. If you die, you'll start over, but you'll keep your card collection. There will also be tiered cards, with rarer and more powerful cards being much harder to obtain.

I'll be showcasing an improved demo on June 15 during Steam Next Fest, including some elements of the roguelite mode. My goal is to release the full game on July 10, hopefully.

So, do yourself a favor: download the demo on Steam, gather up to four friends, grab some drinks, and give it a try in local mode. I'm not just saying this because it's my game. It genuinely makes for an incredibly fun party game.

And while you're there... maybe hit that wishlist button too. 😉

Once I've reworked the trailer and captured some new screenshots, I'll share them in a future blog post and, of course, on Steam as well.

Thanks for reading! ❤️
